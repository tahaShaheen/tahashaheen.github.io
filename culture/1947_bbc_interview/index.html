Interviewed by BBC Radio

Documentary: Pakistan, Partition And The Present

In 2017, I was interviewed by BBC Radio as part of a documentary titled “Pakistan, Partition And The Present”. I spoke of my personal journey of recording Partition eyewitness accounts and why this work was important to me.

Read a summary of the documentary Listen to the 2 part documentary

Intervieweing team was Shahzeb Jillani (BBC correspondent in Pakistan) and Tim Mansel (Producer for BBC World Service).
